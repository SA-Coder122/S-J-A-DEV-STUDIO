About Orel City Company Limited

Building Ghana's infrastructure with excellence since 2010

Our Company

Orel City Company Limited is a leading Ghanaian civil engineering and construction firm, registered with the Ministry of Roads and Highways as an A2B2 contractor. We specialize in the construction of roads, drainage systems, and general civil works.

With over a decade of experience, we have successfully delivered numerous infrastructure projects across Ghana, contributing to national development and improved transportation networks.

Our team of qualified engineers, project managers, and skilled technicians ensures that every project meets the highest standards of quality, safety, and durability.

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver superior civil engineering and construction services that contribute to Ghana's infrastructure development, while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental responsibility.
