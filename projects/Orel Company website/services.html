Our Services

Comprehensive civil engineering and construction solutions for Ghana's development

Road Construction

As an A2B2-classified contractor with the Ministry of Roads and Highways, we specialize in complete road construction projects from initial earthworks to final asphalt paving.

Our Road Construction Services Include:

  • Feeder road construction and rehabilitation
  • Urban and inter-urban road networks
  • Asphalt paving and concrete roads
  • Road widening and improvements
  • Shoulder construction and stabilization
  • Road signage and markings installation

We utilize modern equipment and follow MRH specifications to ensure durability and quality in all our road projects.

Drainage & Culvert Works

Proper drainage is essential for road longevity and flood prevention. We design and construct comprehensive drainage systems for both urban and rural areas.

Drainage Solutions:

  • Stormwater drainage systems
  • Box culverts and pipe culverts
  • French drains and side drains
  • Retention ponds and flood control
  • Bridge approach drainage
  • Erosion control measures

Our drainage works comply with hydraulic engineering principles and local environmental regulations.

Civil & Infrastructure Works

Comprehensive civil engineering services for public and private infrastructure development projects.

Civil Engineering Services:

  • Site preparation and earthworks
  • Retaining wall construction
  • Bridge and culvert construction
  • Water supply infrastructure
  • Parking lots and pavements
  • Utility installation and relocation

We handle projects from initial surveying and design consultation to final construction and handover.

Site Preparation & Earthworks

Proper site preparation is crucial for any construction project. We provide comprehensive earthworks and site development services.

Site Preparation Services:

  • Land clearing and grubbing
  • Excavation and grading
  • Compaction and soil stabilization
  • Cut and fill operations
  • Erosion control installation
  • Site drainage establishment

We have the equipment and expertise to prepare sites of all sizes for construction.
