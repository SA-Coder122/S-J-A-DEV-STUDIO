About Great Peace & Love Hotel

Discover our story of serenity and excellence

Our Story

Great Peace & Love Hotel was founded on a simple philosophy: travelers deserve a sanctuary where comfort meets reliability. Located in the serene Ashalaja, Greater Accra Region, our 10-bedroom hotel has become the trusted choice for business and leisure travelers seeking authentic peace.

With a consistent 9.3/10 rating from guests worldwide, we've built our reputation on stellar staff service, calm environments, and unwavering commitment to guest satisfaction.
