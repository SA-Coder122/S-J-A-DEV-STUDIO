Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer emergency after-hours service?

Yes, we maintain a fully staffed 24/7 dispatch center. Our on-call technicians are equipped with fully stocked vehicles to handle critical emergencies at any hour, minimizing damage to your property.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Every lead technician holds a Master Plumber license. Additionally, EliteFlow carries a $5M umbrella liability insurance policy to ensure complete protection for our high-end commercial and residential clients.

Do you provide warranties on your work?

We provide a 2-year workmanship warranty on all labor, which is double the industry standard. Parts and equipment are covered by their respective manufacturer warranties, which we manage on your behalf.
